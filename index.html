Supply and Demand Scales

What keeps chips in short supply vs what can make prices fall

🔴 Prices Stay High or Go Up

  • Yield issues (factories making too many defective chips)
  • Huge demand for AI servers
  • Factories keep focus on HBM lines

🟢 Prices Fall or Stabilise

  • Yield improvements (defect rates drop fast)
  • Too many chips in the market (oversupply)
  • Building new memory factories

Conclusion: 3 Traffic Lights (Signals) for Investors

A simple guide using facts and data, not guesses

1. Monitor Big Tech investment plans

Monitor whether Google, MS, Meta and Amazon cut budgets for servers and data centres

2. Track daily DRAM price trends

Track daily spot prices from small dealers, as they change before contract prices do (*Note: use DRAMeXchange)

3. Track supply expansions by main suppliers

Observe whether other suppliers get chip approvals or convert more factory lines to HBM, which increases supply
